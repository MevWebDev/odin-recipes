Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Seasonings
  • Tikka Masala sauce

Steps

  1. Begin by slicing the chicken.
  2. Season it to your taste.
  3. Heat up some oil in a pan.
  4. Combine the chicken with the oil and seasonings, ensuring an even mix.
  5. While that's cooking, prepare your rice separately.
  6. Once both components are ready, place them on a serving dish.
  7. Generously ladle Tikka Masala sauce over your creation. Enjoy!
